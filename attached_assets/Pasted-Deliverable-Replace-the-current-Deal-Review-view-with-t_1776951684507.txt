Min-width 240px. Max-width 300px for most. Keywords tooltip gets up to 400px wide with prose.
Tooltips positioned above the element with 6px gap.

9. Keyword detection dictionary (for the Keywords pill)
Classify each listing's pubCmt + agtCmt concatenation:
HIGH signals (any of these → level = High, wrap in <span class="kw">…</span>):

AS IS, AS-IS
MOTIVATED, MOTIVATED SELLER
PRICE REDUCED, PRICE REDUCTION
VACANT, DELIVERED VACANT
PROBATE, ESTATE SALE, AFFIDAVIT OF DEATH
DIVORCE
MUST SELL, NEEDS TO SELL
CASH ONLY, CASH PREFERRED
NO CONTINGENCIES

MID signals (no HIGH signals, but any of these → level = Mid):

FIXER, NEEDS TLC, TLC, HANDYMAN SPECIAL
REHAB, NEEDS WORK, PROJECT
SUBJECT TO INSPECTION
NO RENT CONTROL

LOW — no signals detected → level = Low.
Implementation: Simple string matching is fine for v1. Case-insensitive. Wrap every match in <span class="kw">…</span> before injecting into the tooltip.

10. Rules (non-negotiable)

No Text or Email buttons on the card surface. Communication goes through the drill-down or the post-call nudge.
No priority dots next to the task title. Removed.
Phone icon triggers the nudge. Don't auto-send text/email until AA clicks Yes.
All tooltips are white. Never dark.
Status chip is one unified pill — never split % and stage into two elements.
Sections render in order: Active & Off Market → Pending/Backup/Hold → Closed/Expired/Canceled.
Auto Tracker is a menu item, never a left-side icon.
Don't remove existing functionality. This reorganizes the surface — all current pages, tools, and logic stay intact behind the drill-down.
